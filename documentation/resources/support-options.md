# Support options

## Immediate Assistance

Hitting an issue with Hook0? We're here and ready to help! Day or night, browse or search our Knowledge Base to find the fastest answers.

## Email Support

We offer email support in English for all customers between 9am-6pm Central European Time (CET). Requests must use account-associated email addresses. Users should check spam folders if no response appears.

## Priority Support

Priority support is included in all Pro and Enterprise plans. Reduced response times based on severity levels apply for eligible customers using account email addresses.

### Initial Response Time

| Severity | Pro | Enterprise |
|----------|-----|-----------|
| Sev 1 (Critical) | 4 business hours | Contact sales |
| Sev 2 (High) | 1 business day | Contact sales |
| Sev 3 (Normal) | 2 business days | Contact sales |
| Sev 4 (Low) | 2 business days | Contact sales |

**Priority Support Availability:**
- Pro: 9am-6pm Monday to Friday CET
- Enterprise: Contact sales for details

### Severity Definitions

**Sev 1 - Critical:** Critical impact/complete outage. Majority of users are impacted. There is no workaround. Customer's business is dramatically impacted. Example: Hook0 is inaccessible for the entire company applications.

**Sev 2 - High:** Severe impact or downgrade of services. Majority of users are impacted. A workaround may be available, but performance is degraded. Example: Large number of users can't access Hook0 services.

**Sev 3 - Normal:** Restricted impact, most of the system is functioning properly. Something is not functioning as expected. Example: An application, a subscription or subset of users are experiencing poor latency when using Hook0 API.

**Sev 4 - Low:** Low impact or informational request. Feature requests. Example: Product usability issues or use case inquiries.

We know how important it is to resolve issues quickly, and that's why we make all reasonable efforts to meet or exceed the stated response and resolution times for issues within our scope of support.
