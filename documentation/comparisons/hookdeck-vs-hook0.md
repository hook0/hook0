# Hookdeck vs Hook0

[Hookdeck](https://hookdeck.com/) is a webhook proxy and management platform that offers features such as proxying webhook requests, reducing duplicates, and managing webhooks in a central dashboard.

**Legend:** ✅ = Full support | ⚠️ = Partial support | ❌ = Not available

## Feature Comparison

| Feature | Hookdeck | Hook0 |
|---------|----------|-------|
| **SaaS** | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Self-hosting** | ❌ Cannot self-host Hookdeck | ✅ Fully self-hostable |
| **Open-Source** | ❌ | ✅ |
| **Self-funded** | ❌ $5.5M total (seed $2.65M, Apr 2022) | ✅ Fully self-funded |

*Funding data last verified: Dec 2024*
